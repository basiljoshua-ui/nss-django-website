Who We Are

Developing Youth for Community Service

The National Service Scheme (NSS) is an Indian government-sponsored public service program conducted by the Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports. It provides an avenue to the youth for community service and developing their personality through voluntary work.

At College of Engineering Poonjar, Unit 99 is a vibrant community of passionate students dedicated to social causes, ranging from environmental conservation and health camps to rural development and youth empowerment.
